Raisin Oatmeal Cookies

Nutrition : 193 calories,
5 stars - based on 433 reviews
Yield : 10

Ingredients

  • 1 orange with rind
  • 6 tsp. safflower oil
  • 3/4 c. honey
  • 1/2 c. skim milk
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 c. whole wheat pastry flour
  • 1 1/2 c. rolled oats
  • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt, low salt or less
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 c. raisins

Directions

  • Grind orange with rind in grinder or blender; add oil, honey, milk and vanilla and blend.
  • Combine remaining ingredients; add to orange mixture.
  • Stir to blend.
  • Drop by large tablespoonfuls onto Teflon baking sheet.
  • Bake at 300u00b0 for 25 to 30 minutes.
  • Remove to wire racks to cool.
