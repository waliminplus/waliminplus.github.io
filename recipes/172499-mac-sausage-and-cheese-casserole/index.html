Mac Sausage And Cheese Casserole

Nutrition : 297 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 388 reviews
Yield : 9

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb. elbow or shell macaroni
  • 2 cans Campbells Cheddar cheese soup
  • 1 can milk
  • 1 lb. sausage link smoked, turkey, Polish, etc., sliced like pepperoni
  • 1 1/2 c. shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tsp. hot sauce optional
  • 1/4 c. bread crumbs

Directions

  • While cooking macaroni, simmer soup, milk and hot sauce in a medium saucepan stirring often.
  • When smooth, add 1/2 cup shredded cheese to sauce and stir in.
  • Drain macaroni but do not rinse and place them in casserole dish.
  • Mix in 1/2 of sausage slices.
  • Pour and mix in cheese sauce.
  • Top with remainder of sausage slices and sprinkle with remaining cup of shredded cheese.
  • Sprinkle bread crumbs over top.
  • Cover dish and bake at 350u00b0 for 40 minutes. Serve hot.
  • Serves 6 to 8.
