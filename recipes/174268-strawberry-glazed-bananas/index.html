Strawberry Glazed Bananas

Nutrition : 194 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 151 reviews
Yield : 2

Ingredients

  • 1 10 oz. pkg. frozen sliced strawberries, thawed
  • 2 Tbsp. sugar
  • 2/3 c. water, divided
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp. cornstarch
  • 1/8 tsp. red food coloring
  • 1 Tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 lb. bananas

Directions

  • Combine strawberries, sugar and 1/3 cup of the water in a saucepan. Heat until boiling. Stir in cornstarch that has been dissolved in the remaining 1/3 cup water. Cook and stir until mixture boils. Reduce heat and simmer 3 minutes, stirring. Remove from heat; add food coloring and lemon juice. Peel and slice bananas. Add sauce and gently stir to coat bananas. Serve at room temperature. Yield 5 to 6 servings.
