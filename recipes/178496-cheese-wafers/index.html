Cheese Wafers

Nutrition : 300 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 411 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 c. flour
  • 1/8 tsp. red pepper
  • 2 sticks margarine
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 8 oz. sharp cheese, grated
  • 4 c. Rice Krispies

Directions

  • Cream grated cheese and margarine; mix salt, pepper and flour. Add to cheese mixture and mix well.
  • Add Rice Krispies and mix. Roll in walnut size balls.
  • Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten balls with fork about 1/4 inch thick.
  • Bake at 375u00b0 about 10 minutes.
