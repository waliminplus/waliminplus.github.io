Garden Pizza

Nutrition : 294 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 78 reviews
Yield : 5

Ingredients

  • 1 large and 1 small can crescent rolls
  • 1/3 c. Miracle Whip
  • 1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
  • 1 envelope Hidden Valley dressing mix
  • your choice of vegetables
  • grated cheese

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350u00b0.
  • Place crescent roll dough on cookie sheet, making a crust, shaped like a pizza crust.
  • Bake dough in oven at 350u00b0 for 8 to 10 minutes.
