Turban Fillets

Nutrition : 210 calories,
3.9 stars - based on 150 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 4 4 oz. flounder fillets
  • salt and pepper
  • 8 medium chopped shrimp
  • 4 Tbsp. backfin crabmeat
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • 3 or 4 Tbsp. grated Mozzarella cheese
  • melted butter
  • Hollandaise Sauce
  • paprika

Directions

  • Sprinkle fillets with salt and pepper.
  • Set aside.
  • Mix shrimp, crabmeat, scallions and cheese and spread on fillets.
  • Roll fillets into a turban and secure with wooden picks.
  • Place turbans on end in a well-greased baking dish.
  • Brush generously with melted butter.
  • Bake at 350u00b0 for 15 to 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
  • Remove from oven and drain off all liquid.
  • Top the seafood with your favorite Hollandaise Sauce and sprinkle with paprika.
  • Individual dishes work best for this recipe.
  • Serves 4.
