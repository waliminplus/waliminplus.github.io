Ham And Noodle Casserole

Nutrition : 187 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 82 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 1/2 to 1 c. cooked, chopped ham
  • 1/4 lb. American cheese, diced
  • 1 green pepper
  • 5 to 6 oz. uncooked noodles
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 1/2 cans of water
  • 1 tsp. salt

Directions

  • Grease baking dish or casserole.
  • Mix all the ingredients and pour into casserole.
  • Bake, uncovered, for 1 hour at 350u00b0.
