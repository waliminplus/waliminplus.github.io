Fantastic Fudge

Nutrition : 302 calories,
4 stars - based on 34 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 1/3 c. Hersheys cocoa
  • 3 c. sugar
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 2 c. milk
  • 1/3 c. 5 Tbsp. butter or margarine
  • 1 tsp. vanilla flavoring
  • 1 c. chopped pecans

Directions

  • Use butter or margarine to grease lightly an 8 or 9-inch square pan.
  • Thoroughly mix the cocoa, sugar and salt in a heavy 4-quart saucepan.
  • Stir in milk and mix thoroughly with a wooden spoon.
  • Bring mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  • Let the mixture boil, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a temperature of 234u00b0 or until a small amount of the mixture can be formed in a soft ball after being dropped in a cup of cold water.
  • Remove from heat, add butter and vanilla.
  • Do not stir.
  • Cool at room temperature to 110u00b0 pan barely warm to touch.
  • Beat with wooden spoon until fudge begins to thicken and lose gloss.
  • Stir in pecans and quickly spread in the prepared pan.
  • Cool completely.
  • Cut into 1-inch squares.
