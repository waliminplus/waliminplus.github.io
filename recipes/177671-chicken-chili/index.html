Chicken Chili

Nutrition : 222 calories,
4.2 stars - based on 171 reviews
Yield : 3

Ingredients

  • 1 to 1 1/2 lb. cooked, cubed chicken or turkey
  • 2 14 oz. cans chicken broth
  • 3 16 oz. cans white beans and liquid
  • 1 to 2 c. chopped onion
  • 2 Tbsp. minced garlic
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 4 oz. can chopped green chilies
  • 2 tsp. cumin
  • 2 tsp. oregano
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
  • 2 dashes hot sauce

Directions

  • Saute onion and garlic in 1 tablespoon olive oil until transparent. Add chicken, chilies, broth and beans. Add spices and heat 10 minutes.
  • Serves 4 to 6.
