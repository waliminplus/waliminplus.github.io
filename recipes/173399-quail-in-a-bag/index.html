Quail In A Bag

Nutrition : 261 calories,
4 stars - based on 46 reviews
Yield : 10

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp. flour
  • 6 quail
  • salt
  • ground red cayenne pepper
  • 1 c. chopped onion
  • 1/2 c. chopped bell pepper
  • 1 lb. fresh mushrooms
  • bay leaf
  • 1 c. Sauterne wine
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 c. water

Directions

  • Grease pan.
  • Put 1 tablespoon flour in a large cooking bag and shake well, coating sides evenly.
  • Split dressed birds down the back.
  • Salt and red pepper them.
  • Rub seasonings well into the birds.
  • Put the birds in the bag.
  • Add all the other ingredients to the bag to tie.
  • Punch 12 holes in top of the bag with a 2-tined fork.
  • Bake at 350u00b0 for 45 minutes.
  • Serves 4 to 6.
