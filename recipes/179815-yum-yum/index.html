Yum-Yum

Nutrition : 298 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 285 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 c. cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 c. cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 c. uncooked rice
  • 1 1/2 to 2 c. water
  • 4 Tbsp. soy sauce

Directions

  • Bake potatoes, cut lengthwise and remove contents so as not to break shells.
  • Mash, season with sugar and butter and add chopped nut meats.
  • Refill shells.
  • Cover with finely chopped nuts. Sprinkle lightly with sugar.
  • Reheat in oven for a few minutes.
