Tortilla Beef Bake

Nutrition : 208 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 239 reviews
Yield : 2

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 - 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 1 can chopped green chiles
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 2 1/2 c. crushed tortilla chips, divided
  • 1 16 oz. jar salsa
  • 1 1/2 - 2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
  • onions, optional

Directions

  • Cook ground beef and chiles. Drain.
  • Stir in soup.
  • In a shallow, 2 1/2 quart greased baking dish, sprinkle 1 1/2 cups of tortilla chips.
  • Top chips with beef mixture.
  • Add salsa and cheese.
  • Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or until bubbly.
  • Sprinkle the remaining chips over the top of the casserole.
  • Bake for 5 minutes longer or until chips are brown. Cool for a few minutes before serving.
