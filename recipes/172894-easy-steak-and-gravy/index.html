Easy Steak And Gravy

Nutrition : 218 calories,
5 stars - based on 155 reviews
Yield : 3

Ingredients

  • 1 round steak
  • 1 can French onion soup
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • garlic powder
  • parsley flakes
  • 1/2 soup can water
  • 1 pkg. gravy mix brown

Directions

  • Cut steak into small pieces.
  • Salt and pepper steak and place in crock-pot.
  • Add celery, green onion, parsley flakes and garlic powder.
  • Pour soup and water on top.
  • Cook on low overnight.
