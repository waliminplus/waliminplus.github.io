Cider Beef Stew

Nutrition : 182 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 138 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 3 cups apple cider
  • 2 Tbsp. cider vinegar
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp. dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 medium carrots, cut into 3/4 inch pieces
  • 3 ribs celery, cut into 3/4 inch pieces
  • 2 medium onions, cut into wedges
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water

Directions

  • In a Dutch oven, brown beef in oil;
  • drain.
  • Add cider vinegar, salt, thyme and pepper;
  • bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 1 1/4 hours.
  • Add potatoes, carrots, celery and onions.
  • Return to a boil.
  • Reduce heat;
  • cover and simmer for 30 to 35 minutes or until beef and vegetables are tender.
