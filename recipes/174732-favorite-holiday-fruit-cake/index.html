Favorite Holiday Fruit Cake

Nutrition : 283 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 89 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 3 c. flour
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. soda
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 c. sugar
  • 1 1/2 c. oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 3/4 c. pecans, chopped
  • 3/4 c. raisins
  • 1 8 oz. can crushed pineapple in heavy syrup do not drain
  • 1 8 oz. jar maraschino cherries, halved do not drain
  • 3 bananas, diced
  • 3/4 c. coconut
  • 2 apples, peeled and diced

Directions

  • Sift first 4 ingredients together into a large bowl.
  • Stir sugar into above mixture with a large spoon.
  • Do not use an electric mixer with this recipe; a large spoon is sufficient. Stir in oil, eggs, vanilla and pineapple with spoon.
  • Stir in remaining ingredients.
  • Mix well.
  • Use floured and greased Bundt pans.
  • Sufficient mix for 1 large Bundt pan or 2 small 6 cup capacity Bundt pans.
  • Bake at 350u00b0 for approximately 50 to 60 minutes.
  • Cake may crack on top when done.
  • Test for doneness with sharp knife.
  • Serve warm with butter or whipped cream.
  • Cream cheese frosting can be used on this cake, but it is an excellent cake without frosting.
