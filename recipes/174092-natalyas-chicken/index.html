Natalya S Chicken

Nutrition : 169 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 314 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp. oregano
  • 1 Tbsp. rubbed sage
  • 1 Tbsp. basil
  • 4 chicken breasts, skin removed
  • 1 fresh lemon, 1/2 for juice and 1/2 for slices
  • 3 fresh carrots, sliced
  • 3 stalks fresh celery, chopped
  • 8 mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 3/4 c. butter, sliced and crumbled
  • 1/3 c. water

Directions

  • Combine oregano, sage and basil thoroughly.
  • Wash and pat dry the chicken breasts.
  • Pat chicken breasts with combined spices on both sides.
  • Place in a glass casserole dish 2 to 3 inches deep. Squeeze 1/2 of fresh lemon on chicken.
  • Scatter over the chicken the carrots, celery, mushrooms, tomatoes and lemon slices.
  • Pour 1/3 cup of water over the vegetables and chicken.
  • Distribute butter over top of vegetables and cover tightly with casserole lid or aluminum foil.
  • Bake at 300u00b0 until vegetables are tender, about 1 hour.
  • Serve chicken with vegetables over buttered rice with spinach salad.
  • Serves 4.
