Uncle Bob S Chicken And RiceFoil Cooking For 1 Person

Nutrition : 194 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 174 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken thighs
  • 1/4 c. Adolphus long grain enriched rice 15 minute style
  • 1/2 c. water
  • approximately 1/4 tsp. salt for rice
  • 2 slices green pepper, chopped
  • 3 mushrooms, chopped fresh, not canned
  • 1/4 onion, chopped or 1 bunch green onion tops, chopped approximately 2 inches of top
  • salt and pepper on chicken only

Directions

  • No matter how you like it, peel skin off thighs.
  • Now, if you like, throw skins in bottom of pouch.
  • Next, add rice, water and salt.
  • Mix up vegetables and toss in after they are cut up.
  • Add dash of salt.
  • Stand thighs on top of everything.
  • Salt and pepper thighs.
  • Seal top of foil and place directly on charcoal.
  • Cook no more than 30 minutes.
  • Two points of caution
  • 1 run sharp knife down each side of thigh bone through meat and 2 when foil pouch looks as if it is going to explode, or break the folds, take a very sharp knife point or toothpick and puncture a small hole in pouch at top.
  • If hole is too large, leave toothpick in hole. This is the steam pressure escape valve.
