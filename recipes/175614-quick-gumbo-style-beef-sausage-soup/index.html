Quick Gumbo Style Beef Sausage Soup

Nutrition : 296 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 215 reviews
Yield : 2

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. cooked smoked sausage, cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • 1 1/2 c. water
  • 1 14 1/2 oz. can stewed tomatoes no salt; undrained
  • 1 10 oz. pkg. frozen cut okra
  • 1 tsp. dried thyme leaves
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp. hot pepper sauce
  • 1 c. rice, cooked keep hot

Directions

  • In large saucepan, combine first 6 ingredients.
  • Bring to boil; reduce heat.
  • Cover and simmer for 6 to 8 minutes, or until okra is tender, stirring occasionally.
  • Stir in pepper sauce. Place 1/2 cup rice into 4 individual soup bowls.
  • Ladle equal amounts of soup into bowls.
