San Antonio Chicken

Nutrition : 183 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 71 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients

  • 3 lb. chicken legs
  • 4 Tbsp. flour
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • dash of salt
  • dash of black pepper
  • fresh sage leaves or 1 tsp. dried sage leaves
  • 2 Tbsp. butter or margarine
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 c. tomato juice
  • 2 Tbsp. tomato paste
  • fresh sage leaves for garnish optional

Directions

  • Rinse chicken legs; pat dry.
  • On a plate, combine flour, paprika, salt, black pepper and either freshly crushed sage or dried sage leaves.
  • Dip chicken in flour mixture to coat on all sides.
  • In a skillet, heat butter.
  • Brown chicken on all sides. Transfer to a casserole.
  • Saute onion in same pan until soft, but not brown.
  • Sprinkle over chicken.
  • Combine tomato juice and tomato paste; pour over chicken.
