Lemon Poppy Seed Cake

Nutrition : 257 calories,
4.2 stars - based on 316 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 1 box lemon cake mix
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/8 c. poppy seed
  • 2 pkg. instant lemon pudding
  • 1/2 c. milk
  • 1/2 c. vegetable oil
  • 1/2 c. water
  • 1 tsp. lemon extract

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 325u00b0.
  • Mix together all ingredients and pour into a greased Bundt pan.
  • Bake 60 minutes.
  • Let cool approximately 30 minutes and invert onto a plate.
  • Sift on confectioners sugar and serve.
