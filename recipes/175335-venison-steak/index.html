Venison Steak

Nutrition : 198 calories,
5 stars - based on 272 reviews
Yield : 2

Ingredients

  • Venison steaks
  • Salt and Pepper
  • All purpose flour
  • 2 or 3 Tbsp. olive oil or canola oil

Directions

  • Remove any of the white outer edges that may have been left on with a sharp knife.
  • Place steaks on a large cutting board and tenderize by beating steaks with a knife on both sides or using meat tenderizer.
  • Sprinkle the steaks on each side lightly with salt and pepper.
  • Coat steaks with flour.
  • Heat skillet on medium heat and add
  • oil.
  • Gently place steaks in hot oil and cook on medium heat turning as they brown.
  • Steaks may need to be turned more than once.
  • When steaks are well done and browned nicely, remove to serving platter.
  • Garnish with red peppers cayenne or jalapeno-either fresh or dried.
