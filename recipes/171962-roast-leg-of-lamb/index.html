Roast Leg Of Lamb

Nutrition : 216 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 264 reviews
Yield : 5

Ingredients

  • 5 to 6 lb. leg of lamb
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • 1/4 c. butter
  • 10 cloves garlic
  • salt and pepper
  • garlic powder
  • 20 small, peeled potatoes

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350u00b0.
  • Wash the meat well and set aside.
  • In a small bowl, mix salt, pepper and peeled cloves of garlic.
  • Make 10 incisions in the leg of lamb with a sharp knife and place the garlic cloves in each incision.
  • Place leg of lamb into a roasting pan; pour lemon juice and melted butter over meat and sprinkle with salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste.
  • Place roast in the oven and bake for about 4 hours.
  • Check roast after 1 hour and add 1 cup of water.
  • When meat is half done, add potatoes around the lamb.
