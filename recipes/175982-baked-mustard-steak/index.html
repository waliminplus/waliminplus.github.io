Baked Mustard Steak

Nutrition : 170 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 309 reviews
Yield : 5

Ingredients

  • 6 Tbsp. flour
  • 1 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 6 large cube steaks
  • 8 Tbsp. butter or margarine
  • 1 1/2 c. sliced onions
  • 2 Tbsp. cream or half and half
  • 1 1/2 c. sliced mushrooms
  • 1 1/2 c. beef bouillon
  • 1/3 c. brown mustard

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 325u00b0.
  • Mix salt and flour.
  • Pound 1/4 cup of this mix into the cube steaks.
  • Melt 4 tablespoons of butter.
  • Add cube steaks and brown well.
  • Place steaks in a large baking dish. Melt 2 tablespoons butter.
  • Add onions and cook until tender. Melt 2 tablespoons butter; saute mushrooms.
  • Spoon onions and mushrooms over browned steak.
  • Add remaining flour to butter and drippings remaining in the skillet.
  • Stir in bouillon and mustard and cook, stirring constantly until thick make sure to get out all the lumps.
  • Add cream and mix well.
  • Pour sauce over the vegetables and meat.
  • Cover and bake 1 1/2 hours test after 1 hour to see if it is done.
