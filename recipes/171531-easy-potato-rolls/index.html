Easy Potato Rolls

Nutrition : 274 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 168 reviews
Yield : 10

Ingredients

  • 2/3 c. sugar
  • 2/3 c. Crisco
  • 1 c. mashed potatoes no milk or butter
  • 2 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 pkg. active dry yeast
  • 1 1/3 c. warm water, preferably from cooking potatoes
  • 6 - 6 1/2 c. all purpose flour

Directions

  • In a small bowl, cream sugar and Crisco, add potatoes, salt and eggs.
  • Beat in yeast, 2 cups flour and remaining water.
  • Add enough remaining flour to form a soft dough.
  • Form into a ball, place in a large greased bowl and grease top of dough.
  • Cover and let rise until double, about 1 hour.
  • Punch dough down.
  • Pinch off small pieces to form 1 inch balls.
  • Place 3 in each greased muffin cups.
  • Cover and let rise until double.
  • Bake at 375u00b0 for 20 to 25 minutes then brush with melted butter.
  • Remove from pans to wire racks to cool.
  • Makes about 36 to 45.
