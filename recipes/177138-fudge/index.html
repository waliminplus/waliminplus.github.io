Fudge

Nutrition : 166 calories,
3.9 stars - based on 203 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 2 c. sugar
  • 4 Tbsp. cocoa Hersheys
  • 2/3 c. milk
  • 2 Tbsp. corn syrup
  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • 1 Tbsp. vanilla
  • 1 Tbsp. Marshmallow Fluff

Directions

  • Boil the sugar, cocoa, milk and corn syrup over medium-high heat.
  • When a drop of this mixture in a glass of cold water forms a firm ball, it is cooked.
  • Add the butter, vanilla and Marshmallow Fluff heaping.
  • Stir this over heat until melted. Immerse pan in cold water until it begins to thicken.
  • Pour quickly into greased 9 x 9-inch square pan.
