Mega Tuna

Nutrition : 268 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 145 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 1 6 1/8 oz. can solid white tuna in water, drained
  • 1 2 1/4 oz. can sliced black olives, drained
  • 1 head fresh broccoli, cut into small florets approximately 3 c.
  • 1/2 c. grated low-fat Mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 4 green onions, chopped, or to taste
  • 1/4 c. slivered almonds or unsalted sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 c. low-fat or reduced calorie mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 3 Tbsp. skim or 1% milk
  • 2 tsp. mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • dash of hot pepper sauce

Directions

  • In a large bowl, place tuna, olives, broccoli florets, cheese, tomato, onion and almonds or sunflower seeds.
  • Toss lightly to mix ingredients.
  • In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise or salad dressing, milk, mustard, garlic powder, black pepper and hot pepper sauce.
  • Beat with a fork to blend.
  • Pour over broccoli-tuna mixture; gently fold until ingredients are lightly coated.
  • Chill well.
  • To serve, eat as a salad or use as a sandwich filler in pita breads or buns.
  • Makes 4 servings.
