Stained Glass Cookies

Nutrition : 271 calories,
5 stars - based on 223 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 1 c. sugar
  • 4 Tbsp. butter
  • 1 egg
  • 3 Tbsp. milk
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 1/4 c. flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • crushed hard candy pieces suckers or Jolly Ranchers

Directions

  • Cream the butter and sugar.
  • In a separate bowl, combine the flour and baking powder.
  • In another bowl, beat the milk, eggs and vanilla together.
  • Add the flour mixture alternating with the milk mixture to the butter and sugar beating well after each addition. The dough should be easy to handle; if not, chill it for 1 hour. Give your child some dough. Roll it into skinny snakes.
  • Bend each snake into a design on a foil covered cookie sheet and pinch the ends together. Sprinkle a thin layer of the crushed candy into the enclosed space.
  • Make sure the ends are sealed so melted candy wont leak out. Bake at 375u00b0 for 9 minutes.
  • Allow to cool and peel off the foil.
