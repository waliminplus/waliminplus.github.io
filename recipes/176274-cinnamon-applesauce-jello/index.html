Cinnamon-Applesauce Jello

Nutrition : 224 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 146 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 1 3 oz. pkg. lemon jello
  • 1 c. applesauce
  • 1/3 c. cinnamon hearts

Directions

  • Bring 1 cup water to simmering point.
  • Dissolve cinnamon hearts, stirring to dissolve.
  • Add to jello and stir until dissolved.
  • Add applesauce and chill until set.
  • Serves 4 to 6.
