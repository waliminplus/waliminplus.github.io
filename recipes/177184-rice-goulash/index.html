Rice Goulash

Nutrition : 243 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 261 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 1/2 c. water
  • 1 c. uncooked rice
  • 3 beef bouillon cubes, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
  • 1 medium green pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1 c. shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions

  • Brown beef with onion in 10-inch skillet; drain off fat.
  • Stir in water, rice, crushed bouillon cubes and dry mustard.
  • Bring to boil.
  • Reduce heat, cover and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 25 minutes.
  • Stir in green pepper and tomato.
  • Sprinkle cheese over top.
  • Cover and remove from heat.
  • Let stand 2 to 3 minutes to melt cheese.
  • Makes 4 to 6 servings.
