Clam SauceFor Spaghetti

Nutrition : 262 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 267 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 18 good size clams, 24 littleneck clams or 2 cans minced clams
  • 3 garlic cloves, mashed
  • 10 fresh parsley sprigs leaves only
  • 1/4 c. olive oil
  • 1/4 c. butter
  • pinch of red and black pepper
  • 2 good size tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp. tomato paste
  • pinch of flour
  • 2 Tbsp. clam juice

Directions

  • Chop garlic and parsley together.
  • Combine oil and butter in skillet and heat.
  • Add clams, garlic and parsley and red and black pepper; stir.
  • If using canned clams, add salt.
  • Cook for 5 minutes.
  • Start spaghetti water.
  • Add tomatoes, tomato paste, 2 tablespoons clam juice and flour.
  • Cook for 20 minutes simmer.
