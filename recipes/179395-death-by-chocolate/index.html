Death By Chocolate

Nutrition : 283 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 285 reviews
Yield : 9

Ingredients

  • 1 box 19.8 oz. fudge brownie mix
  • 8 chocolate covered toffee candy bars 1.4 oz.; like Heath or Skor bars
  • 1 12 oz. Cool Whip
  • 3 pkg. 3.5 oz. each instant chocolate mousse

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350u00b0.
  • Make brownies according to mix.
  • Bake brownies.
  • Let cool.
  • Meanwhile prepare mousse according to directions.
  • Break candy bars into small pieces in food processor. Break up brownies into small pieces and place half in bottom of large glass bowl or trifle dish.
  • Cover with half of mousse, then half of candy and then half of Cool Whip.
  • Repeat layers with the remaining ingredients.
  • Serves up to 24.
