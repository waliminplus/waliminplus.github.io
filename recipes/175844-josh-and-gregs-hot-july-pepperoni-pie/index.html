Josh And Greg S Hot July Pepperoni Pie

Nutrition : 261 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 426 reviews
Yield : 3

Ingredients

  • 6 oz. spaghetti, cooked and drained
  • 2 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1/4 c. grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 c. creamed cottage cheese
  • 1 3 1/2 oz. pkg. pepperoni
  • 1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
  • 1 c. 4 oz. shredded Mozzarella cheese

Directions

  • In a 2-quart glass round baking dish, combine spaghetti, eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  • Press mixture against bottom of dish to form the crust.
  • Microwave on High power 1 1/2 to 2 minutes, or until heated.
  • Add cottage cheese; spoon over top of spaghetti.
  • Arrange pepperoni slices over cottage cheese layer.
  • Spread sauce evenly over top.
  • Cover with plastic wrap.
  • Cover; microwave on High power for 7 to 10 minutes, or until mixture is hot.
  • Sprinkle shredded Mozzarella cheese over top.
  • Let stand, covered, for 5 minutes before serving.
  • Yields 6 servings.
