Apple Crisp

Nutrition : 223 calories,
5 stars - based on 167 reviews
Yield : 10

Ingredients

  • 2/3 c. packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 c. flour
  • 1/3 c. uncooked oats
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/3 c. butter
  • 1/3 c. chopped pecans
  • 6 c. peeled apple slices

Directions

  • Combine dry ingredients.
  • Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse meal.
  • Stir in nuts.
  • Layer half of the apples and dry mixture into an 8-inch square pan.
  • Repeat layers.
  • Bake at 350u00b0 for about 30 minutes.
