Lemon-Herbed Turkey Breast

Nutrition : 251 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 430 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients

  • 1 large lemon
  • 1 turkey breast approximately 5 lb.
  • sprigs of fresh herbs such as sage, rosemary, oregano, basil or thyme
  • 2 to 3 sprigs parsley
  • 1 Tbsp. flour

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 325u00b0.
  • With a fork, prick surface of lemon. Place in cavity of turkey breast along with fresh herbs.
  • Place flour in oven cooking bag and shake.
  • Place prepared turkey breast in bag.
  • Close bag securely.
  • Make 6 1/2-inch slits in top of bag to allow steam to escape.
  • Place in a shallow pan large enough to hold turkey breast comfortably.
  • Roast at 325u00b0 for about 2 to 2 1/2 hours, or until a meat thermometer placed in thickest muscle, without touching a bone, reaches 170u00b0.
  • If fresh herbs are unavailable, use 1/4 teaspoon each of dried herbs.
