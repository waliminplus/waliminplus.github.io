Broccoli-Rice Casserole

Nutrition : 190 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 156 reviews
Yield : 9

Ingredients

  • 3/4 c. rice, cooked
  • 2 pkg. frozen chopped broccoli, cooked, or 2 c. fresh, cooked
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 Tbsp. margarine
  • 1/2 c. milk
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 2 c. Cheddar cheese

Directions

  • Saute onion in margarine.
  • Mix and heat milk, soup, and cheese. Mix all ingredients together and bake at 350u00b0 for 25 minutes.
  • May top with onions.
  • Bake another 10 minutes.
