Fruit Cookie Pie

Nutrition : 225 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 109 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 3/4 c. butter or margarine, softened
  • 1/4 c. firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 c. granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 c. all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1 Tbsp. granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 to 2 c. prepared vanilla pudding
  • 1 1/2 c. each sliced kiwi, strawberries, mandarin oranges and raspberries or any fruit you prefer

Directions

  • In medium bowl, beat together the butter and sugars until light and creamy.
  • Add egg and vanilla; beat well.
  • Combine flour, baking powder and salt.
  • Gradually blend into butter mixture until just moistened.
  • Press dough evenly into a 12-inch pizza pan. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of sugar.
  • Bake in a 375u00b0 oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until lightly browned.
  • Remove and cool.
