Bear Dijonnaise

Nutrition : 216 calories,
5 stars - based on 139 reviews
Yield : 10

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 to 5 lb. lean bear meat
  • 4 Tbsp. flour
  • 2 Tbsp. Parmesan cheese, grated
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 to 1/2 lb. lean salt pork
  • 2 whole shallots
  • 2 garlic cloves, unpeeled
  • 1 Tbsp. whole black peppercorns, crushed
  • 9 oz. Dijon mustard
  • 8 oz. honey
  • 3 1/2 c. dry red wine

Directions

  • Trim all fat off bear meat.
  • Be sure to clean out kernel musk gland to avoid gamy taste.
  • Cut meat into 3-inch cubes. Season flour with Parmesan and black pepper.
  • Mix well.
  • Roll meat cubes into seasoned flour.
  • Cover well.
