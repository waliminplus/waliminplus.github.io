Applesauce Raisin

Nutrition : 253 calories,
4.2 stars - based on 18 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 2 c. flour
  • 3/4 c. dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 c. raisins
  • 1 tsp. each nutmeg and baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. each baking soda and salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 c. oil
  • 3/4 c. applesauce
  • 1 tsp. vanilla

Directions

  • In large bowl, mix flour, sugar, raisins, nutmeg, baking powder, soda and salt; set aside in another bowl.
  • Beat eggs, applesauce and vanilla.
  • Pour over flour mixture until moistened. Spread in greased 13 x 9-inch pan.
  • Bake in a 375u00b0 oven for 20 minutes.
  • Cool in pan.
  • Cut in bars.
