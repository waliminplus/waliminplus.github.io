Lemon Chiffon PieHusband Favorite

Nutrition : 274 calories,
5 stars - based on 215 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. sugar
  • 1 envelope unflavored gelatin
  • 2/3 c. water
  • 1/3 c. lemon juice
  • 4 egg yolks, slightly beaten
  • 1 Tbsp. grated lemon rind
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1/2 tsp. cream of tartar
  • 1/2 c. sugar

Directions

  • Blend sugar, gelatin, water, juice and egg yolks in saucepan. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until it boils.
  • Add rind.
  • Place pan in cold water; cool until mixture mounds when dropped from spoon. Fold into a meringue of egg whites, cream of tartar and sugar.
  • Pile into cooled baked pie shell.
  • Chill several hours until set.
