Polish Cucumber RelishFrom M.C.E.S.

Nutrition : 254 calories,
4 stars - based on 346 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 4 c. ground unpeeled cucumber 4 to 5
  • 1 c. ground green pepper about 2
  • 1/2 c. ground sweet red pepper about 1
  • 3 c. ground onion
  • 3 c. finely diced celery
  • 1/4 c. salt
  • 3 1/2 c. sugar
  • 2 c. white vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp. celery seed
  • 1 Tbsp. mustard seed
  • 1 whole garlic, chopped

Directions

  • Use coarse blade on grinder.
  • Combine all vegetables.
  • Sprinkle with salt.
  • Cover with cold water.
  • Let stand 4 hours.
  • Drain in colander, pressing out all excess liquid.
  • Combine sugar, vinegar, seed and garlic.
  • Bring to boil.
  • Stir in vegetables.
  • Simmer 10 minutes.
  • Pack in jars.
  • Process in boiling water bath 10 minutes. Yields 5 to 6 pints.
