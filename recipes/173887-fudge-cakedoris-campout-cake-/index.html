Fudge CakeDoris Campout Cake

Nutrition : 174 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 183 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 2 c. sugar
  • 2 c. flour not sifted
  • 1 c. water
  • 1/2 c. shortening
  • 3 Tbsp. cocoa
  • 1 stick margarine
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 c. buttermilk or 1/2 c. milk and 1 1/2 tsp. vinegar
  • 1 tsp. soda
  • 1 tsp. vanilla

Directions

  • Put sugar and flour into a large mixing bowl.
  • Then put water, shortening, cocoa and margarine into a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  • Pour over dry ingredients and mix.
  • Then add eggs, buttermilk, soda and vanilla.
  • Mix well.
  • Pour into a 9 x 13-inch pan and bake at 400u00b0 for 20 to 25 minutes.
