Basic Cream Pie

Nutrition : 183 calories,
3.9 stars - based on 350 reviews
Yield : 9

Ingredients

  • 2 c. milk
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2/3 c. sugar
  • 1/3 c. flour
  • 1 Tbsp. butter
  • 1 tsp. vanilla

Directions

  • Scald milk; mix dry ingredients together.
  • Add hot milk, stirring constantly.
  • Cook in a double boiler until smooth and thick, about 15 minutes.
  • Beat egg yolks slightly and add a small amount of hot mixture to them.
  • Add this quickly to remainder of hot mixture and cook 3 minutes longer.
  • Cool.
