Lye Soap

Nutrition : 232 calories,
4 stars - based on 360 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 6 lb. fat, melted
  • 1 can lye
  • 2 1/2 pt. hot water

Directions

  • Dissolve the lye in the hot water; let cool, then pour lye solution in a slow, easy stream into melted fat, stirring constantly.
  • Continue stirring until cool.
  • Pour into boxes that have been dipped in cold water.
  • Cut into desired size of squares when cold and set.
  • You can add perfumed bath oil, if so desired.
