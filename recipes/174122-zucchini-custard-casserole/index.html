Zucchini-Custard Casserole

Nutrition : 238 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 353 reviews
Yield : 10

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. zucchini about 4 c., sliced
  • 2 Tbsp. minced onion
  • 1/4 c. melted butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 c. milk
  • 2/3 c. grated cheese
  • dash of pepper
  • 1/3 c. Parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbsp. dry breadcrumbs
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • dash of pepper

Directions

  • In large skillet, saute zucchini and onion in butter until tender.
  • Set aside.
  • In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs and milk. Add half of the grated cheese, half the Parmesan cheese, the breadcrumbs, salt and pepper.
  • Stir in cooked zucchini and onion. Turn mixture into a buttered 1 1/2-quart casserole dish.
  • Sprinkle top with remaining cheeses.
  • Bake uncovered in a preheated 350u00b0 oven approximately 35 to 40 minutes, until lightly browned.
  • Easy and delicious.
  • Enjoy.
