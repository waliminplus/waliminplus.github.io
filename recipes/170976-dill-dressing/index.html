Dill Dressing

Nutrition : 211 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 315 reviews
Yield : 3

Ingredients

  • 1 c. plain yogurt or mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. mandarin peel, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp. mandarin juice
  • 1 Tbsp. dill
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  • Mix together and let stand at least 4 hours.
  • Refreshing as salad dressing for pasta or greens.
  • Also good as dressing for cold chicken or fish.
