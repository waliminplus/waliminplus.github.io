Helen Sue Sanderson S Pecan Pie

Nutrition : 196 calories,
4 stars - based on 173 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 1 c. white Karo syrup
  • 1 c. nuts, chopped
  • 1 stick margarine
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 c. sugar

Directions

  • Mix all ingredients together and pour into 9-inch deep-dish pie shell.
  • Cook at 350u00b0 for 10 minutes.
  • Turn down to 325u00b0 and cook 40 to 45 minutes longer, until almost set.
