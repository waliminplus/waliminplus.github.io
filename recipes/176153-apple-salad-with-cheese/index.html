Apple Salad With Cheese

Nutrition : 296 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 275 reviews
Yield : 2

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. apple cider vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 Tbsp. honey
  • 1/3 c. olive oil
  • 10 c. mixed salad greens
  • 4 oz. crumbled cheese Blue cheese is good
  • 3 large apples, cored and cut into cubes
  • 2 ribs celery, cut in medium size pieces
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced

Directions

  • In a medium size bowl, whisk together vinegar, lemon juice, honey, salt and pepper. Gradually drizzle in olive oil, continuing to whisk until thick and well blended.
