Bill Shields Sauerkraut Salad

Nutrition : 259 calories,
4.2 stars - based on 158 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 2 15 to 15 1/2 oz. cans or pkg. sauerkraut, drained
  • 1 c. chopped green pepper
  • 1 c. chopped green onions white and green parts
  • 1 small can sliced water chestnuts
  • 1 small jar pimentos, chopped or sweet red pepper
  • 1/3 c. water
  • 1/3 c. oil
  • 2/3 c. vinegar
  • 1 1/4 c. sugar

Directions

  • Mix first 5 ingredients in a suitable container for storage. Bring to boil the remaining ingredients.
  • Turn down and simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Pour over vegetables.
  • Mix and cool.
  • Will keep for several weeks.
