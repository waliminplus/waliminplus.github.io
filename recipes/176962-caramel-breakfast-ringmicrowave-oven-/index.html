Caramel Breakfast RingMicrowave Oven

Nutrition : 254 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 127 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. brown sugar
  • 4 Tbsp. butter
  • 1 Tbsp. water
  • 1/4 c. chopped maraschino cherries optional
  • 1/4 to 1/2 c. chopped pecans or walnuts
  • 1 can 8 oz. refrigerated biscuits

Directions

  • In glass measure, mix brown sugar, butter and water. Microwave High 1 minute.
  • Add nuts and cherries; set aside.
  • In 1 or 1 1/2-quart round casserole, place small glass in center open side up.
  • Spoon half of sauce mixture in bottom of dish. Separate biscuits; arrange in overlapping ring around glass.
  • Spoon rest of caramel mixture over biscuits.
  • Microwave High 2 1/2 to 3 minutes.
  • Remove glass; invert on serving plate.
  • Serve warm by pulling sections apart.
  • Serves 4 to 6.
