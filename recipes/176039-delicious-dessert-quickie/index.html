Delicious Dessert Quickie

Nutrition : 190 calories,
4.2 stars - based on 175 reviews
Yield : 10

Ingredients

  • 1 c. brown sugar, sifted
  • 1/2 c. cocoa
  • 2 c. water
  • 1 1/2 c. miniature marshmallows
  • 1 pkg. devils food cake mix
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 c. broken pecans

Directions

  • Blend sugar, cocoa and water in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan. Sprinkle with marshmallows.
  • Prepare cake mix according to package directions, but add vanilla to batter.
  • Spoon batter over the mixture in the pan.
  • Top with nuts.
  • Bake in 350u00b0 oven about 50 minutes.
  • Allow to cool slightly, then cut into serving size pieces and serve, sauce side up.
