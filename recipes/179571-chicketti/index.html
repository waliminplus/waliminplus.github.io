Chicketti

Nutrition : 268 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 396 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 2 8 oz. cans mushrooms
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 c. butter or margarine
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • 2 c. sour cream
  • 1/2 can water chestnuts, sliced
  • 7 oz. spaghetti, broken, cooked and drained
  • buttered bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese

Directions

  • Bone, skin, cook and cube chicken breasts.
  • Preheat oven to 300u00b0.
  • Saute mushrooms in butter.
  • Mix mushrooms, soup, sour cream, water chestnuts and spaghetti together.
  • Place in casserole.
  • Sprinkle with bread crumbs, then Parmesan cheese. Bake 40 to 45 minutes.
  • Freezes well.
  • Makes 8 servings.
