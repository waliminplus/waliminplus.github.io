Tangy Meat Loaf

Nutrition : 177 calories,
4 stars - based on 66 reviews
Yield : 9

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. catsup
  • 2 Tbsp. brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. powdered mustard
  • 4 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp. seasoned salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp. onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 2 Tbsp. finely chopped green pepper
  • 1 1/2 c. Wheat Chex cereal
  • 1 1/2 lb. ground beef

Directions

  • In large bowl combine, catsup, brown sugar, and mustard. Reserve 4 Tbsp. mixture for topping.
  • To remaining mixture add Worcestershire sauce, seasoned salt, onion powder, garlic powder, black pepper, and egg.
  • Blend well.
  • Stir in green peppers and Wheat Chex.
  • Let stand 5 minutes.
  • Break up Wheat Chex.
  • Add ground beef.
  • Mix well.
  • Shape into loaf in shallow baking pan. Bake in 350u00b0 oven for 65 minutes.
  • Spread top with reserved catsup mixture.
  • Bake additional 15 minutes.
  • Makes about 6 servings.
