Hoppin John

Nutrition : 262 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 127 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. dried field peas or black-eyed peas
  • 2-inch piece about 3/4 lb. fatback
  • 1 1/2 qt. water
  • 1 medium onion whole
  • 2 1/2 c. long grain white rice
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  • Wash peas in cold water. Boil fatback in a large pot of water on medium heat for about 30 minutes or until water is low and well seasoned with fatback flavor. Add peas, onion and enough water to cover peas. Cook on low to medium heat for about 1 hour. When peas are soft, season with salt and pepper. Remove onion and add rice. Add more water, if necessary, to cook rice about 5 cups is needed.
  • Cook for another 30 minutes and adjust seasonings. Serves 10.
